Creating a map involves the keyboard for most operations. Probably less intuitive and currently disabling the use of tablets, but makes it fast to create maps once you've learned it. Start a new map by clicking the blue New Map button above.

Enter a name for the first node and then press Enter.

Create a new node by pressing "n" on your keyboard. When you create a new node, you get automatically in text input mode and need to provide a name then press Enter before you could do any other operation.

Link two nodes together by selecting the two nodes (click or tap on the nodes you want to select so they become yellow), and then press "l" on your keyboard. Note that only two nodes can be selected for this operation.

Delete a link between two nodes by first select the two nodes, and then press "u" on your keyboard.

Delete a one or more nodes by first select the nodes you want to delete, and then press "d" on your keyboard.

Edit a name for a node by first select the node, and then press "t" on your keyboard. To get out of the input text mode, press Enter.

Zoom in or out of the map by scrolling up or down with your mouse

Move around in the map by clicking on the map (outside any of the nodes) and hold down the click button while moving your mouse in the direction requested.

Rearrange nodes by clicking on a node, and then start dragging the mouse in any direction.
